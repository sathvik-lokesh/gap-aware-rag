# Aurelia Robotics — Company Overview

Aurelia Robotics is a fictional company founded in 2019 in Lyon, France. It
designs autonomous warehouse robots for cold-storage logistics. The company's
flagship product line is the Frostpath series of mobile picking robots.

Aurelia employs roughly 240 people as of 2025. The CEO is Mara Delacroix, a
former control-systems researcher. The company is privately held and has raised
two funding rounds: a seed round in 2020 and a Series A in 2022 led by Northwind
Ventures.

Aurelia's core differentiator is operating reliably at temperatures as low as
-28 degrees Celsius, where standard warehouse robots suffer battery degradation
and sensor fogging. The company holds three patents related to cold-weather
battery thermal management.
